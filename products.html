    The hospitality portal allows the Guests to: Search or browse matching hospitality equipment Track and manage Hospitality requests and orders Pay online for the orders using a Credit Card Manage the communication with Dealers and Renting customers

    Hospitality Platform enables companies to orchestrate and manage all aspects of hospitality: Online Hospitality Store to Showcase Products and Equipment for Hospitality Manage the Dealer Network and centralized inventory across all locations Simplify and enhance the Customer Hospitality Journey Provide role-based access to information and features Provide API and Interfaces to integrate with enterprise and dealer systems Ability to Import and Export data from different data sources
